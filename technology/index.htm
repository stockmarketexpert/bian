Bain Capital Technology

Leading Companies into the Future

Bain Capital is one of the world’s leading multi-asset alternative investment firms with approximately $185 billion in assets under management. We pioneered the value-added approach to investing and have been at the forefront of technology investing since our founding in 1984.

We have a proven, collaborative strategy to support innovative businesses, accelerate growth and achieve scale. Our team has decades of experience investing behind leading technology businesses globally. Our reach across industries and geographies creates unique advantages for management teams as they enter new end-markets and expand internationally. We believe Bain Capital is the partner of choice for growth companies transforming the future of tech.

Tech is Global

and so is Bain Capital

Bain Capital actively invests in technology businesses around the world. Today, global success stems from a combination of global industry perspective and on-the-ground proximity to local markets. Our firm has offices in 21 cities across the Americas, Europe, and Asia-Pacific. We have made technology investments in over 20 countries around the world.

Why does our global footprint matter? We help our technology companies expand into new markets around the world. Bain Capital’s technology team also facilitates the global diffusion of local knowledge and lessons learned, no matter where our partner companies operate. And our international footprint enables us to identify and invest in frontier entrepreneurial corridors outside of historic innovation hubs.

A more connected and accessible world creates an imperative for technology companies to innovate globally. With teams in major regions around the world, Bain Capital offers a gateway to global markets, seamlessly paired with local insights and relationship networks.
